Smart Traffic System

Juilee Bhombe

Use CCTV cameras installed on junctions to figure out which side has maximum traffic and accordingly adjust duration of red/green light automatically. The proposed system's approach towards providing a solution to this problem will be designing a system that is capable enough to identify density of Traffic in the scene and thus altering its timer accordingly. Deep learning system would enable the system to adaptively analyse the traffic scene. Provide low, moderate or heavy traffic considering traffic density as the major parameter. Special feature includes priority to ambulances and fire trucks.
